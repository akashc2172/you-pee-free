One-sentence thesis

Sunbear learns a cell identity representation that is disentangled from time/condition, then recombines factors to predict missing single-cell profiles across time, sex, and modality.

Core challenge: single-cell assays are destructive, so true longitudinal trajectories per cell are unobservable.

Core strategy: factorized cVAE + sinusoidal time encoding + multimodal alignment constraints.

Why This Problem Is Hard

Slide 2 · Intro · 2.5 min

Measurement constraints

  • A cell is measured once, then destroyed.
  • No direct observation of same-cell temporal changes.
  • Time points are often irregular and condition-mismatched.

Data heterogeneity

  • Condition shifts (sex, batch, perturbations).
  • Modality shifts (RNA, ATAC, methylation).
  • Sparse modality coverage over time (ATAC often sparse).

Method gap the paper targets

  • Most methods handle only one axis well.
  • Sunbear aims to model time + condition + modality jointly.
  • Output: counterfactual profile predictions at single-cell level.

Guiding question

If I hold cell identity fixed, what would this cell look like at another time point, in another sex, and/or in another modality?

Experimental Design + Evaluation Logic

Slide 3 · Intro · 2.5 min

Data used

  • Large mouse embryo scRNA-seq time series (up to millions of cells).
  • Additional RNA time-series datasets for integration checks.
  • RNA+ATAC coassay time points to supervise multimodal alignment.

Main held-out scenarios

  • Missing time point inference.
  • Missing sex at a time point.
  • Missing ATAC at a time point, queried from RNA.
Single-modality objective: reconstruction (ZINB) + KL + adversarial time-invariance pressure.
Multimodal objective: ATAC BCE + KL + embedding alignment + cross-modality translation losses.

Primary metrics

  • Pseudobulk Pearson correlation (held-out profile recovery).
  • AUROC/AUPRnorm for differential or accessibility tasks.
  • LISI for integration quality across time/batch/modality.

Validation chain

Biological pattern

  • Autosomal genes also carry sex-biased trends, not only X-linked genes.
  • Immune-process enrichment appears in macrophage female-biased genes.

Interpretive caution: these are predicted dynamic differences anchored by available snapshots, not direct same-cell longitudinal measurements.

Biological implication

Critical Appraisal: Strengths and Limits

Slide 12 · Synthesis · 1:15 min

Strengths

  • Unified model for time, condition, modality, and batch.
  • Scales to large single-cell datasets.
  • Robust validation design against nearest-time baselines.
  • Generates biologically coherent hypotheses in sparse sampling settings.

Limits

  • No true longitudinal same-cell ground truth exists.
  • TLCC findings are correlative and model-dependent.
  • Multimodal alignment currently benefits from coassay supervision.
  • Assumes relatively smooth temporal transitions.

Best framing: Sunbear is a high-quality hypothesis generator and integrative inference engine, not a direct causal proof system.

Conclusion

Slide 13 · Conclusion · 1 min

What this paper changes

  • Makes counterfactual single-cell temporal profiling practical across sex and modality gaps.
  • Extends from pure accuracy benchmarking to biologically meaningful discovery.
  • Provides a reusable framework for future disease and developmental time-series studies.

Closing line for delivery

Sunbear shows that with careful latent factorization, we can convert disconnected snapshot datasets into coherent, testable temporal stories of cell state regulation.

Suggested transition to Q&A

"I’m happy to discuss baselines, uncertainty, and where this method should be validated next experimentally."

Backup Slide: Quick Formula Reference

Slide 14 · Backup
RNA loss: −ZINB loglik + KL, plus adversarial discriminator for time-invariant cell embedding.
ATAC loss: −BCE + KL, with embedding alignment and translation losses against RNA reference.
Temporal analysis (Fig 4): predict continuous trajectories, shift ATAC curve vs RNA curve, compute lag-wise correlation (TLCC).
